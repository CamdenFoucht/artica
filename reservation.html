Reserve by Phone

We take reservations for breakfast and lunch. To make a reservation, please call us at (800) 111 2222 between 6am-4pm, Monday to Sunday.

We do not book the computer area – we leave this for walk-in guests to ensure that we always offer some tables for those who have not booked.

Private Dining & Events

Whatever your event or celebration, we are here to make your special day a truly memorable one. Reste offers private rooms for groups over eight people with a three course meal. Custom arrangements can be made, please call for more information.
